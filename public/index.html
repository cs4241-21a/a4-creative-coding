Instruction for Music Visualizer!

This music visualizer displays three groups of frequencies: bass, mids, and treble, shown in visual elements from bottom to top.

Controls Instruction!

On the right, you can find the control panel. There are three tracks available, which may be selected using the "Track" dropdown.

There are three sliders: Playback speed, volume, and "Pulse." speed and volume are easy to understand. "Pulse" controls how much the circles increase in size.

The colors folder allows you to pick the color for the three visualizer groups.
